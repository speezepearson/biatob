Hi! This is a site that helps people make friendly wagers, thereby clarifying and concretizing their beliefs and making the world a better, saner place.

Personally, when I force myself to make concrete predictions — especially on topics I feel strongly about — it frequently turns out that I don't actually believe what I thought I did. Crazy, right!? Brains suck! And betting, i.e. attaching money to my predictions, is just an extra incentive to get them right.
